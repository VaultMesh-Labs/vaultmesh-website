Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Architecture

This site is a static edge artifact. Pages are pre-built HTML served from CDN edge nodes. No JavaScript executes on page load. No origin API calls are made during normal browsing.

What we collect

  • Contact form submissions — name, email, subject, and message when you voluntarily submit the contact form. Stored in our receipted intake system.
  • Support tickets — information you provide when opening a support ticket. Stored on the isolated support host.
  • Server logs — standard HTTP access logs (IP, user agent, timestamp, path) retained for security and operational purposes. Rotated and purged on a regular schedule.

What we do not collect

  • No analytics scripts (no Google Analytics, no Plausible, no tracking pixels)
  • No cookies (no session cookies, no tracking cookies)
  • No fingerprinting (no canvas, WebGL, or font enumeration)
  • No third-party resources loaded on page view

Third parties

We do not sell, share, or transfer personal data to third parties for marketing purposes. Server infrastructure is hosted on dedicated hardware (Hetzner, Germany). DNS is managed through Cloudflare.

Data retention

Contact submissions and support tickets are retained for the duration of the engagement plus a reasonable period for follow-up. Server logs are rotated regularly. You may request deletion by contacting the contact form or emailing privacy@vaultmesh.org.

Changes

This policy may be updated. Changes are recorded in the site's build manifest and verifiable through the proof chain.
