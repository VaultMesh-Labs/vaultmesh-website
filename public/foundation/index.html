VaultMesh Foundation

Proof infrastructure governed as a public good.

Legal entity

VaultMesh Foundation is a company limited by guarantee (CLG) incorporated under the Companies Act 2014. The Foundation exists to maintain the proof chain infrastructure, publish verification tools, and ensure long-term continuity of the cryptographic evidence layer. 

entity: VaultMesh Foundation CLG
type: Company Limited by Guarantee
jurisdiction: Ireland
governing_law: Companies Act 2014

Mission

Build and maintain sovereign verification infrastructure where claims are machine-verifiable, evidence is portable, and trust is earned through cryptographic proof. The Foundation ensures that the core proof chain remains open, auditable, and independent of any single commercial interest.

What the Foundation maintains

  • Hash chain continuity (append-only receipt ledgers)
  • Signing key custody and rotation ceremonies
  • Public attestation surface (/attest/)
  • Verification tooling (open, no vendor lock-in)
  • Anchor commitments (RFC 3161, ETH, BTC)

Governance principles

  • No equity — guarantee structure, not shares
  • Proof-gated decisions — receipted, signed, append-only
  • Minimal surface — complexity is the enemy of verification
  • Public accountability — state published, not promised
  • Adversarial validation — distrust is a design input
